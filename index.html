Cooking Never Got Easier

  • Naina
  • 12 Likes
  • 9 Comments

Get an “easy recipes” or “basic recipes” type of cookbook, preferably with a ring binder so you can open it flat on the counter for easy reference. Learn from them the basics, like how to bake, broil and broil various meats and vegetables, how to make the basics like béchamel (white) sauce and tomato sauce. There are many three to five ingredient recipes online, which can give you confidence that you can turn out a tasty product. Baking recipes are like formulas, and must be followed exactly. When you progress with non-baking cooking you can start to experiment with adding your favorite ingredients.
